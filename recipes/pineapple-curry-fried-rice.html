Pineapple Curry Fried Rice

Sweet, salty, umami funk makes this pineapple curry fried rice a go to for our family.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups water
  • 1 Â½ cups uncooked white rice
  • 2 tablespoons Asian fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons pineapple juice
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound boneless chicken meat, cubed
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained

Recipe steps:

Step 1:

Combine water and rice in a saucepan over high heat; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and all liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes. Set rice aside.

Step 2:

Whisk fish sauce, pineapple juice, and curry powder together in a small bowl.

Step 3:

Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat until shimmering. Cook and stir chicken and onion in hot oil until chicken is no longer pink and onions are translucent, about 5 minutes. Combine water and rice in a saucepan over high heat; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and all liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes. Set rice aside.

Step 4:

Stir in cooked rice, pineapple chunks, and curry mixture; cook and stir until rice is hot, 5 to 10 minutes.
